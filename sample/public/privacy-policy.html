This Privacy Policy ("Policy") explains how CPG Business and Finance ("CPG," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects data obtained through our API application ("Software"). The Software retrieves transactional data from QuickBooks Online in JSON format for reporting purposes. By authorizing access to your QuickBooks Online account, you agree to the collection and use of your data in accordance with this Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Transactional Data from QuickBooks Online

  • We collect transactional data directly from QuickBooks Online as authorized by the user.
  • The data is retrieved solely for the purpose of generating reports and is not stored, modified, or used for any other purpose.

1.2 Access and Authorization Data

  • We collect minimal data related to the authorization process, such as access tokens, solely to facilitate secure access to QuickBooks Online data.
  • No personally identifiable information (PII) is stored by our Software beyond what is required for the authorization process.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Data Retrieval and Reporting

  • The primary use of the collected data is to generate secure URLs for retrieving transactional data in JSON format.
  • The data is used exclusively for reporting purposes as authorized by the user.

2.2 Access Management

  • We use access and authorization data only to manage and control the connection between QuickBooks Online and our API application.
  • This information is not used for marketing or any other purposes.

By authorizing access to your QuickBooks Online account, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
